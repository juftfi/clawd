# Integrations Setup Guide Template

## Purpose
Walk through connecting an integration to clawd.bot.

## Structure
1. **Intro** (1 sentence): What this integration enables
2. **Requirements** (bullets): Account/API needs
3. **Setup steps** (numbered): Connection process
4. **Verify it works** (1-2 sentences): Quick test
5. **Common issues** (2 bullets): Troubleshooting tips

## SEO Focus
- Primary: "connect [platform] to clawd"
- Secondary: "clawd [platform] integration setup"

## Tone
Clear, technical but accessible, reassuring

## Example Intro
"Connect WhatsApp to clawd.bot to manage your assistant directly from your favorite messaging app."
