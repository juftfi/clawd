# Integrations Comparison Template

## Purpose
Compare messaging platforms or integration approaches.

## Structure
1. **The question** (1 sentence): What you're deciding
2. **Platform A** (bullets): Strengths/features
3. **Platform B** (bullets): Strengths/features
4. **Best for** (2-3 bullets): Use case recommendations
5. **Bottom line** (1 sentence): Quick guidance

## SEO Focus
- Primary: "clawd WhatsApp vs Telegram"
- Secondary: "best messaging app for clawd"

## Tone
Balanced, helpful, practical

## Example Intro
"WhatsApp or Telegram for your clawd? Here's how to choose based on your needs."
